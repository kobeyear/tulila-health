🎨 Color Psychology for Menopause Care

💜 Recommended: Purple

  • ✅ Medical Authority: Professional healthcare credibility
  • ✅ Empowerment: Strength and confidence for women
  • ✅ Transformation: Perfect for menopause journey
  • ✅ Premium Feel: High-end care positioning

🎯 Alternative Options

  • 🩺 Teal: Classic medical trust (like hospitals)
  • 💼 Navy Gold: Luxury positioning for affluent market
  • 🌿 Emerald: Natural wellness approach
  • ❤️ Coral: Warm feminine energy (might feel less medical)

💡 Recommendation: The original purple strikes the perfect balance of medical authority and feminine empowerment for menopause care. It tests well across demographics and builds trust while inspiring action. The teal is the strongest alternative for pure medical credibility.
