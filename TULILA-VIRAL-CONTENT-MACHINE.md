## 📊 PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZATION

### Viral Detection System

#### **Key Metrics to Track**:
- **View velocity**: >1000 views in first hour
- **Engagement rate**: >5% likes, >2% comments
- **Share rate**: >1% (critical for viral spread)
- **Completion rate**: >70% watch to end
- **Demographics**: Ensure 45-65 women are primary viewers

#### **Viral Triggers** (From Medvi Research):
- **Speed**: Fast-paced education (45-60 seconds max)
- **Relatability**: "This is literally my life" comments
- **Educational value**: "I learned something new"
- **Shareability**: "My mom needs to see this"

#### **Weekly Analysis**:
```
1. Viral Content Review
   - Which videos hit >50k views?

   - What demographics engaged most?

2. Failing Content Analysis
   - What got <1k views?

   - Adjust posting schedule
```
